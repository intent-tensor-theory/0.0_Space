# 1.2 What Cannot Exist in CTS

---

## No points

Points require location. CTS has none.

---

## No adjacency

Adjacency requires "next to." CTS has no topology.

---

## No motion

Motion requires trajectory through space. CTS is pre-spatial.

---

## No ordering by location

Position-based ordering is forbidden.
