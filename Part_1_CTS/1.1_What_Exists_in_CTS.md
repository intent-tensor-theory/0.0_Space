# 1.1 What Exists in CTS

---

## Permission without position

CTS contains permissions that do not require location.

A permission is the capacity for a resolution to occur.

---

## Potential without extent

Potential exists in CTS without requiring spread, volume, or area.

---

## Distinguishability without distance

Two things may be distinguished without being separated.
