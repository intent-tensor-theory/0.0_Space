# 2.3 Collapse and Expansion Operators

---

## + and − as processes, not directions

These operators denote resolution types, not spatial directions.

---

## Collapse as selection, not motion

Collapse is the act of fixing one outcome.

It does not move.

---

## Expansion as permission, not spread

Expansion increases admissible outcomes.

It does not spread through space.
