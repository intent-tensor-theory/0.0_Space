# 2.2 Polarity as Asymmetry

---

## Polarity is not charge

Charge is spatial. Polarity is structural.

## Polarity as recursive imbalance

When one resolution is not interchangeable with another, polarity exists.

---

## Why polarity precedes dimension

Dimension requires ordering.

Polarity requires only difference.
