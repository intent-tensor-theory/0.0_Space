# 2.1 Scalar Intent (Φ)

---

## Φ as non-spatial permission

Φ represents admissible collapse potential.

It is not distributed over space.

---

## Why Φ is not a field

A field exists "in" space.

Φ precedes space.

---

## Why Φ has no coordinates

Coordinates label locations.

Φ has no location.
